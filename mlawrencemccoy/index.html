Contact Me

I enjoy making things. In a way, there’s really not much more to it than that. Sure, I have theories on why I do it. I have theories on art and what it’s for, how it helps us, how it hurts us. If I really thought I could express these ideas with words, I would have chosen to be a writer. Since I’m not a writer, I try to speak visually. I try to be honest with my art and let it express what I really feel is important to me. Hopefully people will enjoy it. Hopefully I’ll never have to stop. Aside from that, I’m a Texan guy in his early 30′s, living in a cottage in the countryside near Austin. If you’re ever out this way, stop on by and say hi. See what I’m up to.
